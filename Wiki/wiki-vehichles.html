# Vehichles Vehichles in stickman stories are a big thing as the war is going on you will need armored vehichles to increase troops survivability in the battlefield, and it is quite expensive to do so. Here you will find all the information about the vehichles used in stickman stories, from cars, to jets, to tanks, and boats. Here will be a list of different vehichles used by redfor and blufor.
